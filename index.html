A community space for learning, creativity and connection.

The Oak Project (TOP) is a small welcoming community project where people can come together to learn useful skills, share creativity, and support one another in a friendly environment.

All groups and classes are online and free to attend.

View weekly timetable

Groups

No experience needed, all welcome, whatever level.

Computer Basics

A friendly introduction to computers email and staying safe and confident online.

Beginner Coding

A gentle introduction to programming where we explore simple coding concepts and small creative projects together.

English Speaking Practice

A relaxed conversation group for people with little or no English as well as anyone who wants to practise speaking with more confidence.

Mindfulness and Meditation

Simple mindfulness practices to help cultivate calm focus and awareness in everyday life.

Art Sharing Group

A welcoming creative space to share artwork ideas and inspiration with optional prompts to explore creatively together.
