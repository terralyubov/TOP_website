Art Sharing Group

This is a welcoming space for anyone who enjoys making or sharing art.

Sessions may include things like:

  • sharing drawings, paintings, writing, music, photography, or crafts
  • talking about art that inspires you
  • optional creative prompts to explore together
  • gentle discussion and idea sharing
  • making space for curiosity and experimentation

You are welcome to share your own work, unfinished work, or pieces by other people that you admire and want to discuss.

The aim is not technical perfection, but creating a supportive environment where people can explore creativity, exchange ideas, and enjoy making things together.

Session details

When: Sunday — 3:30–4:30 pm
Duration: 1 hour
Format: Online (Zoom)

If this time does not work for you, please feel free to get in touch. The Oak Project is still growing, and we hope to add more sessions as the community develops. We will always do our best to accommodate times that work for as many people as possible.
