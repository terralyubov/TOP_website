Help & Support

If you have questions, need help signing up, or would like support joining a group, you are very welcome to get in touch.

WhatsApp: 07593 195218

Email: hello@theoakproject.org

You can send a WhatsApp message or an email if you have questions, need help joining a group, or are unsure where to start.

It is completely fine if you are not confident with technology. Support is available, and no question is too small.

Before you join

All groups are online and free to attend.

No previous experience is needed.

If joining online feels confusing at first, don’t worry — help is available.

Suggestions & getting involved

If you would like to join a group but cannot make the scheduled time, feel free to get in touch. As the project grows we hope to add more sessions and will do our best to accommodate times that work for people.

If you would like to suggest a group, run a session, or get involved in the project, we would love to hear from you.
