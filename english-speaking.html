English Speaking Practice

This is a friendly and informal space to practise speaking English.

Sessions may include things like:

  • everyday conversation practice
  • storytelling or folk tales
  • sharing personal experiences
  • practising for interviews or presentations
  • reading short texts together

People with little or no English are very welcome, and we take things slowly so everyone can feel comfortable joining in.

The group is also open to anyone who would like to build confidence speaking, practise reading aloud, or simply enjoy conversation in a supportive environment.

There is no pressure to speak perfectly. The aim is to create a welcoming space where people from different backgrounds can practise language, share ideas, and build unexpected connections.

Session details

When: Thursday — 5:00–6:00 pm
Duration: 1 hour
Format: Online (Zoom)

If this time does not work for you, please feel free to get in touch. The Oak Project is still growing, and we hope to add more sessions as the community develops. We will always do our best to accommodate times that work for as many people as possible.
