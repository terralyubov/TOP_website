Mindfulness and Meditation

This group offers simple mindfulness practices to support calm, focus, and awareness in everyday life.

Sessions may include things like:

  • guided breathing practices
  • short meditations
  • body awareness and grounding
  • gentle reflection
  • simple tools for managing stress and overwhelm

Everything is optional and beginner-friendly. There is no need for previous experience.

The aim is not perfection, but creating a calm and supportive space where people can explore practices that may help them feel more grounded.

Session details

When: Sunday — 12:00–1:00 pm
Duration: 1 hour
Format: Online (Zoom)

If this time does not work for you, please feel free to get in touch. The Oak Project is still growing, and we hope to add more sessions as the community develops. We will always do our best to accommodate times that work for as many people as possible.
